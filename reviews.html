JESS BELT

As a student I cant afford to go on such long and far away trips, so this new product is perfect for me!  I have always been very curious about all the different cultures and cities around the world but I have never been able to visit them. Now I am able to explore that part of the world for a little price. I recommended this to all my friends who experience the same struggle. It is very interactive and I have never been so excited for a feature like this. It is cool that you can move around so much and can easily go to another place with an new experience. The guides are locally, so you really get the feeling you are actually there. I hope there will come a new update very soon because I cannot wait to explore more!
